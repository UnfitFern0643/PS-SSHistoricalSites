About the beach and John Muir

Seton Sands Beach is a beach with more rocks than sand, also known as a “Rock Beach”, the best way to find out things about the beach and the surrounding area is to go on the “John Muir Way Costal Walk”, which is a tour which takes you from Helensburgh all the way to Dunbar, which passes through Seton Sands Beach. The John Muir Way Costal Walk was created to commemorate the Scottish-American conservationist Mr. John Muir whom aided, and even started making Yosemite Valley become a national park, as well as helping to keep other national parks safe from harm by using his words in his writing to convince politicians to make parks into what we know nowadays as national parks.
